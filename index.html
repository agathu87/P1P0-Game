Ping-Pong

Settings
Players

This is an application that enables a user to enter a number which it then takes and returns a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number with a few exceptions given in the rules of the game. The user can then be able to enter a new number and see new results over and over again.

Rules

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"
